06 / News Headline Mobile App

Spring 2024

An Android application for user’s to discover Local and Top Headlines. A variety of search features include by category, search term, or selecting a map location.

 

Application includes data persistence ⏤ remembering a user’s last search term and category upon launch.

 

 

 

Tech Stack

Kotlin, Google Maps API, NewsApi
